ABOUT US

Building beyond
expectation

SinagPower Technology Philippines Inc. began works as a contractor in the mid-year of 2019. The company started to serve first as manpower for the installation of solar powered establishment and distribution line projects.

The company established its name before the year of 2019 ended. The people behind the company firmly believes that in order to serve a good quality service to the client, a good quality company must be serve first to the employee.

Our main objective as electrical contractor is to help the people economically by promoting the use of renewable energy. We also aim to be a part of an institution that open the opportunity on the secluded area to be reached by the electrical industry. The company aims to build a country wherein electricity is no longer a privilege.

Vision

To be recognized as one of the respected electrical contractor and be known in the electrical industry on promoting natural resources and supporting power industry using renewable energy in the Philippines.

Mission

To construct a solar powered community. To build a transmission and distribution line that aims to reach secluded area in the Philippines. To deliver a good quality service needed by the client. Always
